About Us
Techspardha

Techspardha is the Techno-Managerial Festival of NIT Kurukshetra which started in 1995 as "Technospect" (later changed to "Literati"). It has a variety of events (60+) ranging from paper presentations, robotics software development, aero-modelling, quizzes to managerial events. The year 2013 marked the Golden-Jubilee of NIT Kurukshetra and thus the mega techno-managerial festival was re-christened "TECHSPARDHA".
TS'17 attracted participants from more than 350 colleges across the country.

A Glimpse Into The Future

Inspired by the attempts by vanguards to provide ideas and solutions for the betterment of human life, Techspardha this time comes with the theme Next Generation Technology, providing you a glimpse into the future.

Social Initiatives

Team Techspardha understands its social responsibility towards peoples' issues and takes up various social initiatives every year. In 2017, the social initiative SHE - Save Her - "Beti Bachao, Beti Padhao" was taken up to make people aware that every child should be given equal educational opportunities without any gender bias. Other social initiatives have been, USE ME - to inculcate the sense of public cleaning, SHIKSHA - to provide elementary tuitions to the school children of neighbouring rural areas, HE FOR SHE - to create a world of gender equality, and the list goes on.

Guest Lectures

Abhas Mitra - 19 Jan 2018, 12 pm

One of the most mentioned living Indian Physicist on the web. A TEDx Speaker, an Indian Cosmologist who redefined the Black Hole physics and pulled the attention of physicists at International level. He solved the Black hole Information Paradox before the great cosmologist Stephen Hawking.


Vivek Atray - 19 Jan 2018, 2pm

An alumni of NIT Kurukshetra batch 1984. He is an inspirational speaker, CEO mentor, independent advisor, author and a former IAS. He areas of expertise include: Digital Governance, Investment Promotion, Higher Education, Public Relations, Tourism, Writing and Sports Management.
He has 3 TEDx talks. He speaks on Leadership, Life skills, Pubic Speaking Skills, Emotional Intelligence, Handling Failure and Being a Winner! His novels "Move on Bunny!" & "Dubey ji Bounces Back" proved highly popular. He is writing a book on Life Skills for Young Indians.


Mr Koustubh Phalnikar - 19 Jan 2018, 4 PM

Sr. Deputy General Manager, Product and Technology Development Centre, L&T Defence.
Topic of Discussion:- Excitement in executing Defence Projects.
Mr. Koustubh A. Phalnikar is presently working as SR. DGM- Product & Technology Development in L&T Heavy Engineering at Powai. Mr. Koustubh’s role involves new product development of Land, Naval and Flight Systems such as Missile & Rocket launchers, Torpedo launch systems, Artillery & Air Defence guns, Autonomous Underwater and Aerial vehicles and Marine Propulsion. He joined L&T in August 2007. Prior to this, he was based in the USA, for eight years, where he obtained his Master’s Degree in Mechanical Engineering (Florida State University) with a specialization in Fluid Mechanics, and subsequently worked in the area of R&D for the biotech and clinical diagnostic industry in Maryland. He is a co-inventor on five US Patents and one European Patent in the field of electro- chemi-luminescence.


V.K. Raizada - 20 Jan 2018 , 11 PM

He is Executive Director of IndianOil's Refinery & Petrochemical Complex at Panipat, the largest integrated Refinery Complex of its kind in the country. Prior to this , Raizada was working as Executive Director (Technical), Panipat Refinery, overseeing all technical aspects of day to day working of the Mega Refinery. An Electrical Engineer from the Delhi College of Engineering, supplemented with a Senior Management Training from IMI, Delhi and Europe, Raizada has over three decades of experience to his credit in the national and international Hydrocarbon Industry. After joining IndianOil in 1984 as a Graduate Engineer Trainee, he has worked in various capacities at IndianOil's Mathura Refinery in Uttar Pradesh, Panipat Refinery, Haryana and at the Refinery Headquarters, New Delhi.
