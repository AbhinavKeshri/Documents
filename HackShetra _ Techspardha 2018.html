HackShetra is a 24 hour Hackathon organized by the students of National Institute of Technology, Kurukshetra where students from different college come together and hack on hardware and software to give life to their ideas and solves real life problems.

Get ready to grab prizes worth Rs. 25000

About

Stage- 1: Idea Submission
Come up with an idea from provided category of problems in the first 2 hours.

Stage-2: Hacking and Submission
Use open source apis and develop your idea and then finally submit it.
